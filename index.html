Privacy Policy – Utah Connect

Effective Date: June 24, 2026

Utah Connect ("the App") is an internal machine service and monitoring platform developed for employees of Utah Group to report machine issues, communicate with technicians, monitor repair activities, and manage maintenance workflows across company facilities.

By using Utah Connect, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Employee Information

  • Employee ID
  • Employee Name
  • Department Information
  • Designation
  • User Role (Operator, Technician, Supervisor, Admin)
  • Login Credentials

Machine & Maintenance Information

  • Machine Identification Information
  • Factory Location
  • Floor Information
  • Production Line Information
  • Issue/Defect Reports
  • Repair Status
  • Repair History
  • Service Logs

Communication Data

  • Text Messages

Device Information

  • Device Model
  • Operating System Version
  • Application Version
  • Device Identifier
  • Login Activity Information

2. How We Use Information

  • Employee authentication and access control
  • Machine issue reporting and tracking
  • Technician assignment and notification delivery
  • Repair time monitoring and performance analysis
  • Escalation management
  • Service store parts request and return management
  • Operational monitoring and system administration
  • Security, auditing, and compliance purposes

3. Notifications

The App sends in-app notifications related to:

  • New machine issues
  • Technician assignments
  • Repair updates
  • Escalations
  • System announcements

Notifications are used solely for operational and maintenance purposes.

4. Data Storage

Machine reports, images, voice messages, and related maintenance records may be stored on secure cloud infrastructure, including Amazon Web Services (AWS) S3 or other approved storage services.

Reasonable security measures are implemented to protect company and employee data.

5. Access Control

Access to information is restricted based on employee roles:

  • Operator / End User
  • Technician / Mechanic
  • Supervisor
  • Administrator

Users can only access information necessary for their assigned responsibilities.

6. Data Sharing

The App does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Information may be shared only with:

  • Authorized company personnel
  • Internal management teams
  • Service and maintenance departments
  • Technology providers supporting the operation of the App

Only when necessary for business operations.

7. Data Retention

Maintenance records, issue reports, communication logs, and operational data may be retained as required by company policies and operational needs.

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational safeguards to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

However, no electronic storage method is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

9. Employee Responsibilities

  • Maintaining the confidentiality of login credentials
  • Using the App only for authorized business purposes
  • Reporting unauthorized account access immediately

10. Children's Privacy

Utah Connect is intended exclusively for authorized employees and is not designed for use by children under 18 years of age.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted within the App and become effective immediately upon publication.
